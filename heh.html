About the Book

The Lost Element is a fantasy story about a universe ruled by elemental gods. Each world is protected by a different element — Fire, Water, Air, Land, and Light.

But something is wrong. The God of Light is fading, and the balance of the universe is beginning to collapse.

Before his power disappears, he must choose someone worthy to inherit the Light.

He chooses a fifteen‑year‑old boy named Victor.
